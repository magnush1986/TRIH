How tagging works

The Episode Explorer relies entirely on community tagging. Every episode is linked to one or more Periods, Regions, Topics and sometimes a Series.

These tags power all search filters, charts, groupings and statistics across the site. To keep things consistent, the shared Google Sheet uses locked dropdown lists so that categories remain stable and clean.

Period, Region & Topic

These fields use pre-defined multi-select dropdowns. You may choose as many as needed, but you cannot create new values.

Series

The Series field is auto-suggested from the episode title, but you may freely edit it. It is the only column where free text is allowed.

Locked fields

Episode titles, descriptions, dates, IDs and metadata are imported automatically from the official RSS feed and are locked from editing.
