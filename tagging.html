How tagging works

The Explorer relies on community tagging to function. Every episode is connected to one or more Periods, Regions, Topics, and sometimes a Series. These tags power all filters, charts, groupings and statistics across the site.

Series

The Series field is auto-suggested based on the episode title, but you may freely edit or correct it.

Locked columns

Titles, descriptions, dates, episode IDs and all technical metadata are imported automatically from the public RSS feed and are locked for editing.

Only the tag fields described above are editable.
