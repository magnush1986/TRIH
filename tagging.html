How tagging works

The Episode Explorer relies on community tagging. Every episode is linked to one or more Periods, Regions, Topics, and sometimes a Series. These tags power all filters, charts, groupings and statistics across the site.

To keep things consistent, the tagging sheet uses locked dropdown lists. This ensures that categories remain stable across contributions.

Period, Region & Topic

These fields use pre-defined multi-select dropdowns. You may choose as many as needed, but you cannot create new values.

Series

The Series field is auto-suggested from the episode title, but you may freely edit or correct it.

It is the only editable free-text field. All other tagging fields use controlled lists to prevent accidental creation of new categories.

Locked fields

Episode titles, descriptions, dates, IDs, and all technical metadata are imported automatically from the official RSS feed and are locked for editing.

Only the tag fields described above are editable.
