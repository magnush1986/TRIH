This page shows how often each Period, Region and Topic appears in the community-applied tagging. Percentages are calculated against the total number of episodes in the catalogue, and they do not add up to 100% — because an episode may be assigned to several categories.

Note: The charts below reflect the current categorisation of the episodes. They are not an assessment of editorial choices or topic balance — the producers are, of course, free to cover whatever subjects they choose. This is simply a visualisation of what has been made so far.
